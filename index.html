About

My journey into data began with a fascination for turning complex information into structured, queryable assets. This started in bioinformatics, where I engineered a PyMOL plugin to bridge the gap between raw structural data and scientific insight.

This passion for building robust data solutions led me to the enterprise scale. I've architected data platforms in highly regulated environments, including healthcare, where I built the infrastructure to power predictive models and created automated NLP pipelines for compliance.

Today, as a Senior Data Engineer, I focus on architecting large-scale, cloud-native data solutions. Using tools like AWS Glue, PySpark, and Apache Iceberg, I build the governed, scalable platforms that are essential for turning any data, from any domain, into measurable business value.
